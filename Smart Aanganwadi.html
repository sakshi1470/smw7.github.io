Smart Aanganwadi

Mission---Our Strategies:

Smart Anganwadis is an initiative to support the existing government Aanganwadi centres by providing them with holistic training and raising funds for them. We aim to provide maximum benefit to young children, adolescent girl, and women who are dependent for their growth and living on these aanganwadis.

Our Strategies:

  • To conduct survey of anganwadis, so that we can carefully analyse need, strengths, and gaps of their system.
  • To help the aanganwadis with their requirements and providing them with materials like toys, books, art and craft material, music instruments, etc.
  • To understand the cause of the gap in their system and try to resolve it.
  • To identify their strength and enhance it.
  • To provide proper training of pre-school education, food and nutrition, women's health, family planning etc.
  • To raise funds for them to improve their service and infrastructure.
