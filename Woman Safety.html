Women-Safety

Mission---The Problem---Solution

Mission

Our Main mission is to empower women, to provide a life of dignity to victims and survivors of domestic violence, and to make society equitable for women in India.

The Problem

A large section of women is unsafe, even in their homes. They are deprived of basic respect and have to face domestic violence and abuse. Some choose to tolerate this for life and some commit suicide.

Solution

The solution to this issue is to empower women, make them independent, and give them knowledge about their rights. We aim to develop inner strength and confidence in them making them more capable to raise their voice against any injustice. We have initiated various skill development programs for the same cause. The women are taught basic sewing and weaving skills, cooking skills, computer skills, etc as per their knowledge and qualification.
