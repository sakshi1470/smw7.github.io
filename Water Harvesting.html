Water Harvesting

Mission---The Problem---Solution

Mission

To spread awareness about need and method of water harvesting and set-up of water harvesting tank/system in various locations.

The Problem

The water crisis is a very serious issue, especially in Rajasthan and Maharashtra. Farmers are committing suicide because of a lack of water for irrigation. A large section of people doesnâ€™t have access to clean and safe drinking water. People in Villages are facing water insecurity.

Solution

Rain Water harvesting is an answer to this very serious issue. Rainwater harvesting is a technique of collection and storage of rainwater into natural reservoirs or tanks, or the infiltration of surface water into subsurface aquifers (before it is lost as surface runoff). There is a various method of rainwater harvesting. We aim at making people aware of various methods of rainwater harvesting and setting up water harvesting tanks/systems in multiple locations.
