Mission

To protect our mother-nature and environment and spread awareness for the same.

The Problem

To date, humans have only taken from nature. Oceans, river lakes everything is polluted. Trees are being cut ruthlessly, thus resulting in an increase in carbon dioxide in the air. AC, Vehicle, Refrigerators, everything is contributing to the degradation of nature.

Solution

Planting more and more trees, recycling of plastic are few steps which we can take towards preventing our nature. We aim to organize various camps, presentations in schools to spread awareness among the youth and children towards the prevention of nature, where we will educate them about the things harming our environment and how to control it. We aim to organize tree plantation programs in school, colleges where youth and children will be given tree plantation skills, thus increasing the content of oxygen in the environment.
