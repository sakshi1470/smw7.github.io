School Bag

Mission---The Problem---Solution

Mission

To provide the students in rural Indian and underprivileged students of the urban area with basic infrastructure, proper books, and learning material that will help them in pursuing proper education.

The Problem

Most of the schools in rural India and slum areas of urban India lacks basic infrastructure. Children have to sit on floors for long hours (6-8nhours). This creates medical issues like poor eyesight and back pain, posture-related issues in children, resulting in poor concentration in studies.

Solution

We aim to provide the children with a school bag which will contain all the necessary equipment and things they need for their study. It will also act as a bench for them, by providing a comfortable angle. We aim to distribute this kit to the disadvantaged children, to help them make a positive step towards their bright future.
